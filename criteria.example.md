# Review question
How are explainable artificial intelligence methods evaluated in clinical decision support?

# Include
- Human studies evaluating an AI-supported clinical decision.
- Empirical quantitative, qualitative, or mixed-methods research.
- Reports an explainability, interpretability, transparency, trust, or uncertainty outcome.
- Peer-reviewed full paper in English from 2015 onward.

# Exclude
- Pure algorithm performance studies without a human or explainability outcome.
- Editorials, protocols, abstracts, reviews, and conceptual papers.
- Non-clinical domains.

# Decision policy
Return `review` whenever the abstract does not contain enough information to apply a criterion confidently. Never infer missing methodology details.
